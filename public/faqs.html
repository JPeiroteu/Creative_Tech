FAQs


Frequently Asked Questions


  • What is Creative Tech?

    Creative Tech is a course that combines creativity and technology, offering a multidisciplinary approach to develop skills in design, programming, web development, data science, and artificial intelligence.

    Creative Tech utilizes the Project-Based Learning (PBL) method of learning. PBL is an educational approach that places students at the center of the learning process, engaging them in practical and challenging projects that address real-world problems.


  • Who can enroll in the course?

    The course is open to anyone interested in exploring the intersection of creativity and technology. Basic computer literacy is recommended, but no specific background or prior experience is required.


  • How long does the course last?

    The course duration is 3 year (bachelor degree).


  • Are there any prerequisites?

    No, there are no prerequisites for this course. It is designed to accommodate learners from various backgrounds and skill levels.


  • Can I get a certificate upon completion?

    Yes, upon successful completion of the course, you will receive a certificate of completion.


  • Are there any job placement opportunities?

    While we do not guarantee job placements, we provide career support and guidance to our students, including networking opportunities and job search resources.
